# Bhagavad Gita Reader

This site presents Bhagavad Gita verses with Sanskrit text and Hindi and English readings.

Primary interactions:
- Select chapters and verses using the rail controls.
- Copy the current verse page.
- Open the current verse or full Geeta context in ChatGPT.

Canonical pages are represented with query parameters:
- `?chapter=1&verse=1`
